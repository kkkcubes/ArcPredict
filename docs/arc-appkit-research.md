# Arc App Kit Research

## Planned Features

### Deposit

Users deposit USDC into ArcPredict.

### Send

Transfer winnings to another wallet.

### Swap

Swap supported assets into USDC.

### Bridge

Move liquidity into Arc.

### Unified Balances

Display balances from multiple supported chains.
